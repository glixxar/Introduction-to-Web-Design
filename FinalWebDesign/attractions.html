Our Attractions

Centrifugal Drop

Let your heart drop to the floor as you take on the Centrifugal DROP! Get ready for an adrenaline rush of epic proportions! We’ll shoot you up almost 60 metres into the air and finish the ride up with some bungee-like bounces, so get ready to lose your stomach!

Cheesy Wet 'n' Wild

Taking on Wild Cheese Falls? You’re about to embark on a wild boat ride through the Cheesy Falls, the wildest cheese river on the Moon! This ride is all about thrills, spills and cheesy action. After making your way down the river, you’ll enjoy a steep drop… this ride’s for the daredevils, so we can’t promise you won’t get drenched in cheese!

Mascot Meet & Greet

Everyone’s favourite mascot has arrived and are causing chaos at Cheesetopia. Stay on the prowl to capture a special moment with him as he offers out various cheeses to park guests. But watch out! He might just give you the stinkiest cheese you've ever smelt or the greatest cheese of all time, the golden cheese. Come and chase the cheese today!

High Speed Merry-go-round

The High Speed Merry-go-round is fun for the extreme seekers! Spin and spin around on this ride and try your best to hold your food and heart in as you experience g-forces greater than the ones you have experienced when coming here on your spaceship. Jump on for the ride of your life.

Kids Section

For those who have brought kids with them, fear not for the kids section of Cheesetopia is here to save the day. Each child will be assigned a guardian hand-picked for them by the parents. The guardian will be there for all your child's needs for as long as you want to spend your time in Cheesetopia.

UFO Spinner

If you think a ride on our High Speed Merry-go-round was unsatisfying, think again! You’ll hear a bang, feel a rumble and wonder whether there’s an earthquake... but don’t worry, its only the special effects... or is it ? Take a ride on our UFO spinner as you race through the Moon's atmosphere at high speeds.
